Project Idea

FootFit

Overview

This project idea falls into the category of being a sports app. It would specifically focus on the football code of AFL whereby subscribers/users of the app would utilise it in improving their craft and skills in the sport. All the respective eighteen clubs in the league are provided in the app to cater for the club the user follows. Adding onto this, three listed players from each club in the positions (defender, midfielder and forward) would provide their training programs that they do in preparation for a match. They'd also provide a few related drills/kicking practice for the user to be able to utilise in their own setting.
